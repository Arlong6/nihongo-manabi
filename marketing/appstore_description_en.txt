Nihongo Manabi — Your Japanese Study Companion

One-time purchase. No subscriptions. No ads. Free updates forever.

From hiragana to N1, all in one app. Powered by Spaced Repetition (SRS) to help you build real Japanese skills in just 10 minutes a day.

VOCABULARY
800+ curated words across JLPT N5–N1 levels. Flip-card learning with smart SRS scheduling — the app knows exactly when you need to review. Bookmark tricky words to drill your weak spots.

KANA PRACTICE
All 46 hiragana and 46 katakana with quizzes and confusable character hints (never mix up シ/ツ/ン again).

GRAMMAR
170+ grammar points from N5 basics to N1 advanced, each with example sentences and explanations, followed by practice quizzes.

LISTENING PRACTICE
Hear Japanese audio and pick the correct written form from four choices. Five difficulty levels from N5 to N1 — train your ear alongside your eye.

KANJI PRACTICE
Two-way drills: kanji → reading or reading → kanji. Five levels (N5–N1), 10 questions per round.

JLPT MOCK EXAMS
Full mock exams for N5 through N1, mixing vocabulary and grammar questions. Review wrong answers with detailed explanations. Track your exam history.

USEFUL PHRASES
18 travel-ready phrases covering shopping, restaurants, transport, and emergencies — ready to use on your next trip to Japan.

PROGRESS TRACKING
Study streak counter, daily stats, category mastery bars, and weekly activity charts. Daily reminders keep you on track.
