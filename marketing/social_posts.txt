Title: I made a $1 one-time purchase Japanese learning app (no subscriptions, no ads)

Body:

Hey everyone! I'm an indie developer and I just released Nihongo Manabi on the App Store.

I was frustrated with most Japanese apps being subscription-based ($10-20/month), so I built one that's a one-time $1 purchase with no ads and free updates.

**What's inside:**
- 800+ vocabulary words (N5 through N1)
- Full hiragana & katakana practice with confusable character hints
- 170+ grammar points with examples
- Listening practice — hear audio, pick the correct written form
- Kanji drills (kanji→reading & reading→kanji)
- JLPT mock exams for all levels (N5–N1)
- SRS (spaced repetition) scheduling
- Progress tracking with streak counter

**Other features:**
- Dark mode
- 3 UI languages (Chinese/English/Korean)
- Works offline

I'd really appreciate any feedback! I'm actively developing and adding content regularly.
